David Sundland Front-End Web Developer

E-Mail David

David Sundland is a Front-End Web Developer with Blue State Digital, the creative and tech agency for causes and brands looking to transform how they engage people. From Flagstaff to Brussels, Urban Planning to Dog Training, and WordPress to HTML and React, David Sundland has a diverse background that facilitates his work with a wide variety of causes, campaigns, and brands.

David created the District Concerts website, teaching himself WordPress, HTML, CSS, and Python in order to create a resource that was sorely lacking in Washington, DC. The District Concerts site provides detailed information about over 1,000 concerts each month, all within the boundaries of Washington, DC.

To further his understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and to receive training in React, RESTful APIs, Git, Node, and MongoDB, David recently completed an intense, 6-month Full-Stack Web Development program via Thinkful.

Prior to working in web development, David's career included work both in municipal government and as an entrepreneur. As a city planner, he worked in tight-knit teams, both as a team member and as a team leader. The review process required working closely with people from other disciplines to ensure that developments met all municipal standards. As an entrepreneur, David built his own business and worked closely with dog owners to allow them to solve vexing behavior problems.
